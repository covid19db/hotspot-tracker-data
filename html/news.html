17 Sep 2020 - OxCOVID-19 Tracker

We introduce our interacive Oxford University COVID-19 Tracker of confirmed cases of the UK regions and the World. The above map draws on epidemiology and administrative data from University of Oxford OxCOVID19 Database.

30 Aug 2020 - The OxCOVID19 Database on medRxiv

Our new preprint describing the OxCOVID19 Database is out on medRxiv (doi: https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.08.18.20177147). OxCOVID19 Database links open source data for #covid19 with a single interface at regional level: epidemiology, government response, climate, human mobility, socio-economic data.

12 Aug 2020 - Collaboration with CoMo Consortium

The OxCOVID19 Project started a collaboration with the COVID-19 International Modelling Consortium (CoMo Consortium) to provide data for the CoMo model. To facilate this, members of the CoMo consortium (Ernest Guevarra, Chris Mercado, Robert Medina) have written an R API to the Oxford COVID-19 Database.

21 August 2020 - The OxCOVID19 Database on medRxiv

Our new preprint describing the OxCOVID19 Database is out on medRxiv (doi: https://doi.org/10.1101/2020.08.18.20177147). OxCOVID19 Database links open source data for #covid19 with a single interface at regional level: epidemiology, government response, climate, human mobility, socio-economic data.
